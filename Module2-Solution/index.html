Top 3 Branded Mobiles

Samsung

Samsung Electronics is a South Korean company and a very trusted brand. With its cutting edge-technology and smart features Samsung mobile phones and Smartphone have become highly popular all around the world. Currently the Company Holds 1St Ranking in Top 10 Best Mobile Phone Brands in the World with 31.38% Global Market Share (as in 2019).

Apple

Apple Inc is one of the Top Electronic Companies in USA. It was the first company to launch the world’s first smartphone by the name of iPhone using its own Operating System named iOS. Currently the Company Holds 2Nd Ranking in Top 10 Best Mobile Phone Brands in the World with 22.4% Global Market Share (as in 2019).

Huawei

Huawei is a Chinese Multinational Company and Manufacturers mobile phone devices for its own Brand. Currently, over 80% of the world’s top 50 telecom companies work with Huawei. Popular Huawei Mobile Phone and Smartphone Models are – Huawei P20 Pro, Huawei P20, Huawei P Smart (2019).
